<h3><span
class="mw-headline"
id="2008:_Career_beginning_and_formation">2008:
Career
beginning
and
formation</span>
<span
class="mw-editsection">[<a
href="/wiki?title=Fun_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2"

2008:

<p>Nate
Ruess's
band,
The
Format,
split
in
February
2008.
Immediately
afterward,
Nate
Ruess
asked
Andrew
Dost
and
Jack
Antonoff
to
join
his
new
project.
Dost
had
toured
with
The
Format
and
provided
various
instrumentation,
and
Ruess
met
Antonoff
after
The
Format
toured
with
Steel
Train.<sup
id="cite_ref-4"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup>
The
three
began
working
together
in
<a
href="/wiki/New_Jersey?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="New
Jersey">New
Jersey</a>
within
a
week.
Ruess
sang
melodies
while
the
other
two
provided
music
for
them.<sup
id="cite_ref-about_5-0"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-about-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup>
The
first
demo
song
the
band
recorded
was
&quot;Benson
Hedges&quot;,
which
was
made
available
free
in
<i><a
href="/wiki/Spin_magazine?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Spin
magazine"
class="mw-redirect">Spin</a>'</i>s
August
20,
2008
article
on
the
band.<sup
id="cite_ref-6"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup>
Fun
then
approached
Steven
McDonald,
who
produced
<a
href="/wiki/The_Format?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="The
Format">The
Format</a>'s
album
<i><a
href="/wiki/Dog_Problems?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Dog
Problems">Dog

with
Ruess,
to
produce
their
debut

McDonald
was
enthusiastic
about
the

and
stated,
&quot;I
can’t
believe
what
we’re
working
on
here.
This
crushes
anything
I’ve
ever
done.&quot;<sup
id="cite_ref-about_5-1"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-about-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
