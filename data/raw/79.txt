<hgroup>










<h2
class="rm">Biography</h2>










<h3
class="remove-top-margin">Basildon,
Essex,
United
Kingdom
(<span
itemprop="foundingDate">1980</span>
–
present)</h3>









</hgroup>








</header>








<div
class="wiki">









<div
id="wikiAbstract"
class="wiki-abstract"
data-require="music/wiki">










<div
class="wiki-text">











Depeche
Mode
are
an
English











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/synthpop"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">synthpop</a>
band,
founded
in
1980,
originally
from
the
town
of
Basildon,
Essex,
United
Kingdom.
They
are
one
of
the
most
enduring
and
successful
bands
to
have
emerged
during
the
80s,
and
particularly
from
the











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/new%20wave"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">new
wave</a>/










<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/new%20romantic"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">new
romantic</a>
era.
The
band
name
is
derived
from
a
French
fashion
magazine,
D&eacute;p&ecirc;che
mode,
which
means
“fashion
dispatch.”











<span
class="hide">
<br
/><br
/>
The
band
have
been
highly
influential
in
the
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/electronic"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">electronic</a>
dance
music
scene,
especially
synthpop,
techno
and
trance,
in
part
due
to
their
innovative
work,
recording
techniques
and
use
of
sampling.
Their
most
successful
album
is
arguably
1990’s
<a
title="Depeche
Mode
-
Violator"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Depeche+Mode/Violator"
class="bbcode_album">Violator</a>,
which
reached
the
#2
position
in
the
UK,
#7
in
the
US,
and
sold
over
8
million
copies
worldwide.
The
band
has
released
a
long
series
of
high-charting
singles
over
their
three-decades
long
career.
<a
title="Depeche
Mode
–
Enjoy
the
Silence"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Depeche+Mode/_/Enjoy+the+Silence"
class="bbcode_track">Enjoy
the
Silence</a>
is
their
most
recognized
song
and
is
one
of
the
most
covered
songs
in
history.
<br
/>
<br
/>
The
original
line-up
was
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Gahan"
class="bbcode_artist">Dave
Gahan</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Martin+L.+Gore"
class="bbcode_artist">Martin
L.
Gore</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Andy+Fletcher"
class="bbcode_artist">Andy
Fletcher</a>
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Vince+Clarke"
class="bbcode_artist">Vince
Clarke</a>.
In
December
1981,
Clarke
left
the
band
and
later
went
on
to
form
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Yazoo"
class="bbcode_artist">Yazoo</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/The+Assembly"
class="bbcode_artist">The
Assembly</a>
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Erasure"
class="bbcode_artist">Erasure</a>.
Alan
Wilder
joined
the
group
initially
in
January
1982
as
a
live
keyboardist
but
became
an
official
member
in
December
1982.
He
left
mid-1995
to
fully
concentrate
on
his
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Recoil"
class="bbcode_artist">Recoil</a>
project
and
the
band
continued
as
a
trio.
In
2010
Wilder
re-joined
Depeche
Mode
on
stage
after
16
years,
for
a
performance
of
<a
title="Depeche
Mode
–
Somebody"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Depeche+Mode/_/Somebody"
class="bbcode_track">Somebody</a>
during
a
show
at
London’s
Royal
Albert
Hall
in
aid
of
the
Teenage
Cancer
Trust.
</span>
