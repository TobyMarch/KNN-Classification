<p>To
celebrate
the
10th
anniversary
of
Pedro
the
Lion’s
classic
album
<i>Control</i>,
David
Bazan
has
announced
a
massive
tour
on
which
he’ll
be
playing
the
album
in
its
entirety.
</p>








<p>It
has
already
been
announced
that
Pedro
the
Lion’s
entire
discography
will
be
re-released
on
vinyl

being
out
of
print
for

years.
Up

Aug.
31,

can
pre-order
a

all
five

a
limited

slip

10&times;10

are

to

individually.
Click
<a
href="http://www.undertowstore.com/artist/david-bazan">here</a>
for

pre-orders
will
be

to
arrive
by
Oct.
30.</p>









<p>Take
