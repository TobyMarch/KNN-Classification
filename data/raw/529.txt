<h3

<div

<div
id="wikiAbstract"

<div
class="wiki-text">











Curtis
James
Jackson
III
(born
July
6,
1975
in
South
Jamaica,
Queens,
New
York),
better
known
by
his
stage
name
50
Cent,
is
a
Grammy
award
nominated
rapper,
actor,
singer,
entrepreneur,
author
and
founder
of
the
hip
hop
group
and
label











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/G-Unit"
class="bbcode_artist">G-Unit</a>.
After
leaving
drug
dealing
to
pursue
a
rap
career,
he
released
his
debut
album
Guess
Who’s
Back?
in
2002.
He
was
discovered
by











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Eminem"
class="bbcode_artist">Eminem</a>
and











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Dr.+Dre"
class="bbcode_artist">Dr.
Dre</a>
and
then
signed
to
Interscope
Records.
He
has
since
released
2003’s
Get
Rich
or
Die
Tryin’,
2005’s
The
Massacre,
2007’s
Curtis
and
2009’s
Before
I
Self
Destruct.











<span
class="hide">
<br
/><br
/>
50
Cent
has
engaged
in
feuds
with
other
rappers
including
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Jay-Z"
class="bbcode_artist">Jay-Z</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Ja+Rule"
class="bbcode_artist">Ja
Rule</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/The+Game"
class="bbcode_artist">The
Game</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Cam%27ron"
class="bbcode_artist">Cam’ron</a>,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Fat+Joe"
class="bbcode_artist">Fat
Joe</a>,
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Rick+Ross"
class="bbcode_artist">Rick
Ross</a>.
He
has
also
pursued
an
acting
career,
appearing
in
the
semi-autobiographical
film
Get
Rich
or
Die
Tryin’
in
2005,
the
Iraq
War
film
Home
of
the
Brave
in
2006,
and
Righteous
Kill
in
2008.
50
Cent
is
one
of
the
richest
hip-hop
performers,
having
a
net
worth
estimated
at
US
$440
million
in
2008.<br
/>
<br
/>
Before
50
Cent
ever
inked
a
major
deal,
he
built
up
a
substantial
fan
base
in
New
York
through
underground
mixtapes.
50
Cent
met
up
with
Jam
Master
Jay
of
Run-DMC
and
was
signed
to
his
label
to
write
all
of
Jay’s
music.
After
he’d
left
JMJ,
he
signed
to
Columbia
Records
in
1996.
On
this
label,
he
released
Power
of
the
Dollar,
and
the
singles
How
to
Rob
and
Ghetto
Qu’ran.
Columbia
Records
released
him
from
the
label
after
being
shot
on
May
24th,
2000.
