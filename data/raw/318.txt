<h2><span
class="mw-headline"
id="Death">Death</span>
<span
class="mw-editsection">[<a
href="/wiki?title=Dobie_Gray&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2"
title="Edit
section:
Death">edit</a>]</span></h2>








<p>On
December
6,
2011,
Gray's
official
website
stated
that
he
had
died.<sup
id="cite_ref-15"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-15"><span>[</span>15<span>]</span></a></sup>
According
to
the
<a
href="/wiki/Associated_Press?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Associated
Press">Associated
Press</a>,
he
died
at
his
home
in
<a
href="/wiki/Nashville,_Tennessee?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Nashville,
Tennessee">Nashville,
Tennessee</a>
after
a
long
battle
with
<a
href="/wiki/Cancer?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Cancer">cancer</a>.<sup
id="cite_ref-16"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup>
Sources
differed
as
to
his
age;
his
official
site
stated
he
was
71,
while
the
AP
stated
he
was
69.</p>
