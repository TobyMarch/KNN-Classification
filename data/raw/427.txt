who
was
raised
in
Rincon,
Georgia.
He
currently
lives
in
Nashville,
Tennessee.
He
has
four
brothers
and
two
sisters.
He
is
single
and
has
one
adopted
son.










<br
/>











<br
/>
He
released
his
debut
album
in
2003
featuring
the
powerful
top
ten
hit
Walk
A
Little
Straighter
and
the
top
5
smash
I
Got
A
Feelin.










<br
/>











<br
/>
Billy
has
been
helped
by
the
duet
“Party
for
Two”
with
Shania
Twain
to
reach
his
current
success.











<span
class="hide">
<br
/><br
/>
In
2005
he
released
the
first
single
from
his
second
studio
album,
Must
Be
Doin’
Somethin’
Right
which
reached
the
#1
in
