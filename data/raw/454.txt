<br
/>











<br

Early

<br

Dierks
Bentley

New

attended

Lawrenceville
School,
graduating
in
1993.[1]

{who

/>

/>
Bentley
worked

of
country
performances.
In
2003,

Nashville
released
Bentley’s
self-titled
debut
album,
which

his
successful
first
single,
“What
Was
I
Thinkin’?,”
which
went
to
number
1

Bentley’s

“Come

“Settle

“Free
&amp;

(Down
the

I

“Every

a
Memory.”<br
