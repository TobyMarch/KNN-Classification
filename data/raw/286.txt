length

affectionately

the

to

conducted
by
others.

<p>The
Back

in

concert
on
March
8<sup>th</sup>
at
Emmanuel
Church,
15
Newbury
Street,
at
8

“Bach
and
Beyond,”
looks
back
to
highlights

Bach
motets,
Der
Geist

unser
Schwachheit
auf
and
Singet
dem
Herrn
ein
neues
Lied!
As
Music
Director
Scott
Allen
Jarrett
observes,
“The
sheer
energy
that
bounces
off
the
page
of
a
Bach
motet
approaches
a
level
similar
to
the
collision
of
atomic
particles.”
