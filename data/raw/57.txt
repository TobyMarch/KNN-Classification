<div
id="panel_bio">









Cary
Brothers
is
an
indie
singer-songwriter
from
Los
Angeles
who
first
gained
national
attention
for
his
song
&quot;Blue
Eyes&quot;
on
the
Grammy-winning
&quot;Garden
State&quot;
Soundtrack.
&nbsp;Brothers
recently
released
his
second
album
&quot;Under
Control,&quot;
which
premiered
at
#1
on
the
iTunes
Singer-Songwriter
Chart.
&nbsp;As
one
of
the
most-licensed
artists
in
film
and
television,
he
has
had
over
65
songs
featured
in
shows
like
&quot;Grey's
Anatomy,&quot;
&quot;Bones,&quot;
and
the
recent
hit
movie
&quot;Easy
A&quot;
in
addition
to
appearances
on
&quot;Jimmy
Kimmel
Live!&quot;
and
&quot;The
Late
Late
Show.&quot;
He
is
the
co-creator
of
The
Hotel
Cafe
Tour
and
has
toured
worldwide
with
artists
such
as
Imogen
Heap,
Sara
Bareilles,
and
The
Fray.
&nbsp;In
the
electronic
dance
music
world,
Brothers
has
found
success
collaborating
with
DJ
Tiesto
on
a
club
remix
of
his
song
&quot;Ride&quot;
as
well
as
original
material
for
Tiesto's
latest
collection
&quot;Kaleidoscope.&quot;
This
sampler
includes
a
cover
of
Level
42's
&quot;Something
