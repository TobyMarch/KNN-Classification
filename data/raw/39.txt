<p><a
href="http://consequenceofsound.net/artist/bloc-party/"
target="_blank">Bloc
Party</a>
have
announced
the
release
of
a
new
EP
featuring
five
new
songs.
The
material
appearing
on
<em>The
Nextwave
Sessions</em>,
due
out
August
13th
via
Frenchkiss
Records,
was
written
amidst
the
band’s
2012
tour
behind
their
fourth
LP,
<a
href="http://consequenceofsound.net/2012/08/album-review-bloc-party-four/"
target="_blank"><em>Four</em></a>.
The
first
two
tracks,
“Ratchet”
and
“Obscene”,
were
produced
by
Dan
Carey
(Bat
For
Lashes,
Hot
Chip).</p>








<p>“Ratchet”
serves
the
EP’s
lead
track,
and
will
premiere
this
afternoon
on
BBC
Radio
1.
<strong>Update:</strong>
Stream
the
song
and
watch
its
corresponding
video
below.</p>








<p
style="text-align:center;"><span

block;"><iframe

frameborder="0"></iframe></span></p>








<p>Earlier
this
month,
Bloc
party
guitarist
Russell
Lissack
<a
href="http://arts.nationalpost.com/2013/06/11/bloc-party-plans-indefinite-break-after-summer-festival-circut/"
target="_blank">suggested</a>
that
the
band
would
take
a
hiatus
following
the
EP’s
release
and
remainder
of
their
<a
href="http://blocparty.com/#live"
target="_blank">world
tour
dates</a>.</p>
