Eric
Clapton
(born
Eric
Patrick
Clapton
in
Ripley,
Surrey,
England
on
30
March
1945),
nicknamed
“Slowhand”,
is
a
Grammy
Award
winning
English
composer,
singer
and











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/guitarist"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">guitarist</a>
who
became
one
of
the
most
respected
artists
of
the
rock
era,
winning
three
inductions
into
the











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/rock%20and%20roll%20hall%20of%20fame"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">rock
and
roll
hall
of
fame</a>.











<br
/>











<br
/>
Clapton’s
musical
style
has
changed
during
his
career,
but
has
remained
rooted
in
the











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/blues"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">blues</a>.











<span
class="hide">
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/+noredirect/Clapton"
class="bbcode_artist">Clapton</a>
is
credited
as
an
innovator
in
several
phases
of
his
career,
which
have
included
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/blues-rock"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">blues-rock</a>
with
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/John+Mayall+&amp;+The+Bluesbreakers"
class="bbcode_artist">John
Mayall
&amp;
The
Bluesbreakers</a>
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/The+Yardbirds"
class="bbcode_artist">The
Yardbirds</a>
(1963-1965),
early
single
hit
“Come
With
Me”
by
The
Roosters,
blues-hard
rock
(with
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Cream"
class="bbcode_artist">Cream</a>),
and
work
as
a
sideman
and
a
solo
artist;
he
has
achieved
chart
success
in
genres
ranging
from
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/delta%20blues"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">delta
blues</a>
(Unplugged)
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/psychedelic%20rock"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">psychedelic
rock</a>
(Sunshine
of
Your
Love)
to
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/pop"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">pop</a>
(Change
the
World)
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/reggae"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">reggae</a>
(I
Shot
the
Sheriff).<br
/>
<br
/>
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Eric+Clapton"
class="bbcode_artist">Eric
Clapton</a>
was
a
friend
of
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/George+Harrison"
class="bbcode_artist">George
Harrison</a>
of
the
Beatles.
It
is
believed
that
he
wrote
<span
class="spotify-inline-play-button
inline-play-button"
data-uri="spotify:track:0ISJRL9WlukULH3fX9NfAi"></span><a
title="Eric
Clapton
–
Layla"
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Eric+Clapton/_/Layla"
class="bbcode_track">Layla</a>
about
Harrison’s
wife,
Pattie
Boyd,
whom
he
married
in
1979
after
she
divorced
George
Harrison.
They
divorced
in
1988.
Eric
Clapton
has
also
dated
singers
Marianne
Faithfull
and
Rosanne
Cash,
actresses
Patsy
Kensit
and
Sharon
Stone,
and
rock
muses
Cynrinda
Fox
and
Geraldine
Edwards,
who
was
one
of
the
inspirations
for
Penny
Lane
in
Almost
Famous.
He
is
now
married
to
Melia
McEnry
and
has
been
since
2002.
They
have
three
daughters
together.
