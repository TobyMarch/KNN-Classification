<h2><span
class="mw-headline"
id="History">History</span>
<span
class="mw-editsection">[<a

<p>Following
the
demise
of
the
critically
and
commercially
successful
<a
href="/wiki/Jane%27s_Addiction?qsrc=3044"

Addiction</a>,

<a
href="/wiki/Perry_Farrell?qsrc=3044"

title="Perry
Farrell">Perry
Farrell</a>
and
drummer
<a
href="/wiki/Stephen_Perkins?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Stephen
Perkins">Stephen
Perkins</a>
formed
Porno
for
Pyros
after
acquiring
guitarist
<a
href="/wiki/Peter_DiStefano?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

DiStefano</a>
and
bass
player
<a
href="/wiki/Martyn_LeNoble?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

LeNoble</a>.
Farrell
named
the
band
after
viewing
an
ad
for
fireworks
in
a
pornographic
magazine.<sup
id="cite_ref-2"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup>
The
name
has
also
been
connected
through
song
lyrics
to
the
<a
href="/wiki/LA_riots?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

class="mw-redirect">LA
riots</a>
which
occurred
in
the
band's
hometown
around
the
time
of
Porno
for
Pyros
inception.<sup
id="cite_ref-3"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup>
Prior
to
releasing
their
<a
href="/wiki/Porno_for_Pyros_(album)?qsrc=3044"

(album)">eponymous

Pyros

time

debut

was

in
1993,

the
project
was
enough
to
briefly
drive
the
album
to
the
No.&nbsp;3
position
on
the
<a
href="/wiki/Billboard_Magazine?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Billboard
Magazine"
class="mw-redirect">Billboard</a>
top
200
list.
Reaction
to

was
mixed,
with
some
fans
ecstatic
and
others
disappointed<sup
class="Template-Fact"
style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a

<a
href="/wiki/MTV?qsrc=3044"

title="MTV">MTV</a>.
Following

Porno
for
Pyros
continued
a
heavy

including

at
<a

onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

along

href="#cite_note-ram-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup>

(including,

<p>For
