<p><b>Jefferson

formed

1970s
by

of

rock

<a

the
same
Jefferson
Starship
name.
The
current
Jefferson
Starship,
led
by
co-founder
<a
href="/wiki/Paul_Kantner?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

Kantner">Paul
Kantner</a>,
more
closely

its

psychedelic
and
electric
folk
music
than
the
pop-driven
tunes
it
was
widely
known
for
in
the
early
to
mid-1980s.
It
is
not
to
be
confused
with
<a
href="/wiki/Starship_(band)?qsrc=3044"

<a

(singer)">Mickey

that
also
periodically
tours.
The
latter
group
is
most
frequently
identified
with
the
1980s
pop
tunes
of
the
Jefferson
Starship.</p>
