and











<a

class="bbcode_artist">Soundgarden</a>

gained
in
popularity,
and,
as
a
result,











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/alternative%20rock"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">alternative
rock</a>
became
a
dominant
genre
on

and
Canadian
radio
and
music
television
during
the
early-to-mid
’90s.











<span
class="hide">
<br

/>
Nirvana’s
initial
incarnation
consisted
of
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Kurt+Cobain"
class="bbcode_artist">Kurt
Cobain</a>
on

<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Krist+Novoselic"
class="bbcode_artist">Krist
Novoselic</a>,

<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Chad+Channing"
class="bbcode_artist">Chad
Channing</a>.
After
a
brief
stint
as
a
four-piece
in
1989
with
the
addition
of
second
guitarist
<span
title="Unknown
artist"
class="bbcode_unknown">Jason
Everman</span>,

the

the

found

that
would

the

of
the
group

added

<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Scream"
class="bbcode_artist">Scream</a>

<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Dave+Grohl"
class="bbcode_artist">Dave
Grohl</a>.<br
/>
<br
/>
As
Nirvana’s
frontman,
Cobain
found

the

“spokesman

with
Nirvana
the
“flagship
band”
of
“Generation
X”.
Cobain
was
uncomfortable
with
the
attention,
and
placed
his

on
the

challenging

with

<a

to
shed
their
audience.
While

their

dark
interior,

<a

class="bbcode_artist">Meat
Puppets</a>,

Cobain
had
always
idolised,

<a
