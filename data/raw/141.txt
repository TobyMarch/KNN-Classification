<h3><span
class="mw-headline"
id="Formation_.281997.E2.80.932001.29">Formation
(1997–2001)</span>
<span
class="mw-editsection">[<a
href="/wiki?title=Interpol_(band)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2"
title="Edit
section:
Formation
(1997–2001)">edit</a>]</span></h3>








<p>The
band
was
formed
by
Kessler
and
Drudy.
Kessler
had
&quot;been
looking
to
put
a
band
together
for
a
while&quot;
when
he
met
Drudy.
Kessler
&quot;had
a
very
hard
time
finding
musicians
to
play
with—musicians
at
all,
really.&quot;<sup
id="cite_ref-Pitchfork_9-0"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-Pitchfork-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup>
Kessler
met
Dengler
in
a
philosophy
class
at
<a
href="/wiki/New_York_University?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="New
York
University">New
York
University</a>
and
asked
him
if
he
played
an
instrument.
Later,
Kessler
ran
into
Banks
(whom
he
had
first
met
in
France)
in
New
York
City's
<a
href="/wiki/East_Village,_Manhattan?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="East
Village,
Manhattan">East
Village</a>,
and
the
pair
discussed
collaborating.
Banks
admitted
that
he
and
Dengler
&quot;butted
heads&quot;
early
on
in
the
band's
history,
but
told
<i><a
href="/wiki/Spin_(magazine)?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Spin
(magazine)">Spin</a></i>
that
now
the
two
are
&quot;really
tight,
in
a
spiritual
way&quot;.<sup
id="cite_ref-Night_10-0"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-Night-10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup>
The
band
had
trouble
choosing
a
name
at
first.
&quot;I
got
to
the
point
where
I
was
like,
'Guys,
we're
getting
decent
crowds,
but
like...
we
don't
have
a
name
so
no
one
knows
who
to
go
see
again,'&quot;
Kessler
said.<sup
id="cite_ref-Pitchfork_9-1"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-Pitchfork-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup>
Furthermore,
the
band
considered
Las
Armas<sup
id="cite_ref-Pitchfork_9-2"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-Pitchfork-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup>
and
The
French
Letters
as
names
before
adopting
Interpol.</p>








<p>In
2000,
after
releasing
the
<i><a
href="/wiki/Fukd_ID_3?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Fukd
ID
3">Fukd
ID
No.&nbsp;3</a></i>
<a
href="/wiki/Extended_play?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Extended
play">extended-play</a>
album,
Drudy
left
the

to
focus
on
<a
href="/wiki/Hot_Cross?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Hot
Cross">Hot

and
his
label
<a
href="/wiki/Level_Plane_Records?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Level
Plane
Records">Level
Plane
Records</a>.
Kessler
recruited
Fogarino,
who
worked
at
a
local
vintage
clothing
store
and
at
the
time
considered
retiring
from
music,
to
replace
Drudy.<sup
id="cite_ref-Night_10-1"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-Night-10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup>
While
a
member
of
Interpol,
Drudy
was
also
a
member
of
seminal
first-wave
screamo
act
<a
href="/wiki/Saetia?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Saetia">Saetia</a>.</p>

<h2><span

<p>In
early
2007,
Fogarino
joined
with
former
<a
href="/wiki/Swervedriver?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Swervedriver">Swervedriver</a>
frontman
<a
href="/wiki/Adam_Franklin?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Adam
Franklin">Adam
Franklin</a>
to
form
a
side-project
band
called
The
Setting
Suns.
Since
then,
the
duo
have
changed
their
name
to
<a
href="/wiki/Magnetic_Morning?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Magnetic
Morning">Magnetic
Morning</a>
and
released
a
six-track
EP
on
iTunes.</p>








<p>On
August
4,
2009,
Banks
released
his
solo
first
record
titled
<i><a
href="/wiki/Julian_Plenti_is..._Skyscraper?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Julian
Plenti
is...
Skyscraper">Julian
Plenti
is...
Skyscraper</a></i>
under
the
assumed
name
Julian
Plenti.<sup
id="cite_ref-27"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-27"><span>[</span>27<span>]</span></a></sup>
Although
not
a
long
departure
from
Interpol's
sound,
the
album
features
a
wider
range
of
material,
and
&quot;establishes
Banks'
viability
as
a
musician
outside
of
the
context
of
Interpol&quot;.<sup
id="cite_ref-28"
class="reference"><a
href="#cite_note-28"><span>[</span>28<span>]</span></a></sup>
The
album
was
recorded
at
the
Seaside
Lounge
in
Brooklyn
and
at
<a
href="/wiki/Electric_Lady_Studios?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Electric
Lady
Studios">Electric
Lady
Studios</a>
in
Manhattan.
It
was
mixed
by
<a
href="/wiki/Peter_Katis?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Peter
Katis">Peter
Katis</a>
at
Tarquin
Studios
in
Connecticut.
The
album
was
released
on
<a
href="/wiki/Matador_Records?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Matador
Records">Matador
Records</a>.
He
released
his
second
solo
album,
<i><a
href="/wiki/Banks_(Paul_Banks_album)?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Banks
(Paul
Banks
album)"
class="mw-redirect">Banks</a></i>,
on
October
22,
2012,
under
his
own
name.</p>








<p>In
2013,
Fogarino,
touring
Interpol
member
<a
href="/wiki/Brandon_Curtis?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Brandon
Curtis">Brandon
Curtis</a>
and
<a
href="/wiki/Duane_Denison?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Duane
Denison">Duane
Denison</a>
formed
EmptyMansions,
which
released
their
album
&quot;snakes/vultures/sulfate&quot;
on
April
2,
2013,
on
<a
href="/wiki/Riot_House_Records?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Riot
House
Records">Riot
House
Records</a>.</p>
