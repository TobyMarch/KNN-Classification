<h2
class="rm">Biography</h2>










<h3
class="remove-top-margin">Queens,
New
York
City
(<span
itemprop="foundingDate">1985</span>
–
1998,
2006
–
present)</h3>









</hgroup>








</header>








<div
class="wiki">









<div
id="wikiAbstract"
class="wiki-abstract"
data-require="music/wiki">










<div
class="wiki-text">











A
Tribe
Called
Quest
was
an
influential
hip
hop
group
of
the
1990s,
originally
formed
in
Queens,
New
York
City
in
1985.
The
group
is
composed
of











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Q-Tip"
class="bbcode_artist">Q-Tip</a>,











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Phife+Dawg"
class="bbcode_artist">Phife
Dawg</a>,
and
DJ











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Ali+Shaheed+Muhammad"
class="bbcode_artist">Ali
Shaheed
Muhammad</a>.
A
fourth
member,











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Jarobi+White"
class="bbcode_artist">Jarobi
White</a>,
was
an
intermittent
collaborator
appearing
on
their
first
album,
but
he
left
the
group
soon
after,
although
he
is
mentioned
as
a
‘sometimes’
member
on
their
third
album
liner
notes.
Along
with











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/De+La+Soul"
class="bbcode_artist">De
La
Soul</a>
and











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Jungle+Brothers"
class="bbcode_artist">Jungle
Brothers</a>,
the
group
was
a
central
part
of
the











<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Native+Tongues"
class="bbcode_artist">Native
Tongues</a>
Posse,
and
enjoyed
the
most
commercial
success
out
of
all
the
groups
to
emerge
from
that
collective.











<span
class="hide">
<br
/><br
/>
Q-Tip
and
Phife
had
grown
up
together
in
Queens,
and
met
Muhammad
in
high
school.
The
group’s
name
was
coined
by
The
Jungle
Brothers,
whose
members
attended
the
same
school.
Soon
after,
the
group
began
performing
live
and
recording
on
a
local
label.
The
group
became
a
part
of
the
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/native%20tongues">Native
Tongues</a>
family,
a
Hip-Hop
artist
collective
also
consisting
of
the
groups
Jungle
Brothers,
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/De+La+Soul"
class="bbcode_artist">De
La
Soul</a>
and
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Black+Sheep"
class="bbcode_artist">Black
Sheep</a>.<br
/>
<br
/>
At
the
time
of
their
inception,
A
Tribe
Called
Quest
was
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/unique"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">unique</a>
within
the
hip-hop
community
for
eschewing
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/gangsta%20rap"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">gangsta
rap</a>
and
macho
posturing.
Their
lyrics
focused
on
abstract
and
social
issues
such
as
the
word
“nigger”,
date
rape
and
consumerism.
Musically,
the
group
helped
pioneer
the
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/tag/jazz-rap"
class="bbcode_tag"
rel="tag">jazz-rap</a>
style
(along
with
groups
such
as
<a
href="http://www.last.fm/music/Gang+Starr"
class="bbcode_artist">Gang
Starr</a>
and
De
La
Soul).
They
made
their
studio
debut
in
1990
with
People’s
Instinctive
Travels
and
the
Paths
of
Rhythm.
</span>
