<p>No

<p>The

<a
href="/wiki/David_Crosby?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

Crosby">David
Crosby</a>.
Young

(Erteg&uuml;n

<h3><span

<p>With

Fifth

<p>However,

1968
Palmer's

This

In

<a

<a

<p>The

<a
href="/wiki/Long_Beach_Arena?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Long

class="mw-redirect">Long

Arena</a>
on

After

extended
20plus-minute

“Bluebird”

<a
href="/wiki/Swan_song?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

song">swan

Buffalo
Springfield
disbanded
a

more
than

<p>After
the
group's
breakup,
Furay
and
Messina
compiled
various
tracks
recorded
between
mid-1967
and
early
1968
into
a
third
and
final
studio
album,
titled
<i><a
href="/wiki/Last_Time_Around?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Last
Time
Around">Last
Time
Around</a></i>.
Although
it
featured
Furay's
touching
ballad
&quot;Kind
Woman&quot;,
Young's
classic
&quot;I
Am
a
Child&quot;
and
Stills'
subtle
political
&quot;Four
Days
Gone&quot;,
only

few
of
the
songs
included
more
than
two
or
three
members

group

Even
the

was
a
montage,
with
Young's
image
added
to
a
group
profile
of
the
other
four
members.
Stills
and
Furay
appeared
on
more
tracks
than
any
of
the
others,
essentially
dominating
the
album,
but
it
did
not
light
up
the
charts.</p>
