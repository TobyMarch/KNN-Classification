In

<a
href="/wiki/Eagles_(band)?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Eagles
(band)">Eagle</a>
<a
href="/wiki/Glenn_Frey?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

bought

he

class="mw-headline"

<p>As

<h3><span
class="mw-headline"

<p>Seger

<a

Records">Cameo-Parkway

Though

name
&quot;The
Last

originally

Omens

Criers

&quot;East

Pep

Following
&quot;East

Story&quot;,

four
more

the
James
Brown-inspired

&quot;Sock
It

Me

<a
href="/wiki/Bob_Dylan?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"

Dylan">Dylan</a>-esque
&quot;Persecution
Smith&quot;,
&quot;Vagrant
Winter&quot;,
and

&quot;<a
href="/wiki/Heavy_Music?qsrc=3044"
onmousedown="triggerActionLog('primaryContent','','center','click','primaryContent','','');"
title="Heavy
Music">Heavy
Music</a>&quot;,
released
in
1967.
&quot;Heavy

which

more

&quot;East
Side
Story&quot;,
had

to

suddenly
went
out

business.
It
was
actually
a
top
100
hit
in
Canada,
where
it
topped
out
on
the
national
RPM
charts
at
#82;
in

100,

&quot;bubbling

chart
at
#103.
The

Seger's
live
act
for
many
years
to

<p>The

Seger's
&quot;<a

<i><a

an

Award

Original

category
the
